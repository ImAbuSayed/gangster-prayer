Duniya de fareb ton main bach ke nikalda haan, burning down
Main jitda haan dushmana ton, taking the crown
Wafa de is painde ch dhokhe main khanda haan, feeling the pain
Main hasda haan sab agge, crying in rain
Par tu meri rooh vich enni dhoongi ae, deeply rooted
Main poojda haan tenu sachchi, completely undisputed
Sadi akh milan nu vekh loki sarde ne, making them stare
Main sirf tera ranjha haan, taking full care
