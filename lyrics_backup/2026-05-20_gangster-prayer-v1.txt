[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Kaali rutt ch vi chamke, your beautiful glow
Main takda haan tainu, moving so slow
Iss dil nu main rokda haan, but I lose control
Meri dhadkan te tera haq, claiming my soul

[Hook 2]
Kise hor nu na vekha, you are the one
Main rukda nahi hun, shining like sun
Mere saaha ch wasdi, taking my breath
Main warda haan tenu pyar, ignoring death

[Verse 1]
Raat de is pehar ch teri awaaz sunnda haan, hear your voice
Main kalla is bheed ch tainu labhda haan, no other choice
Kaale sheesheya de pichhe apne hanju lukaunda haan, hide my tears
Main manzil vallo wad-da haan, conquering fears
Tere khayalan vich hi khoya rehnda haan, late in the night
Mere hanere raahan di tu hi ae, my guiding light
Iss shor-sharabe vich main chup rehanda haan, crave your touch
Main har waqt tainu chahunda haan, missing you much

[Pre-Chorus]
Naina de raste tu dil ch utar gayi, creeping in
Main aapna hosh gwaunda haan, a beautiful sin
Dil diya dharkana tainu samjhaunda haan, trying to show
Main mudna nahi chaunda, just go with the flow

[Chorus]
Ni tu dil di oh rani ae, my heartbeat
Main marda haan tere te, making me weak
Tere nakhre main jar-da haan, feeling so high
Main ud-da haan tere layi, reaching the sky
Tere mukhde da chanan ae, driving me crazy
Main warda haan jaan teri te, vision is hazy
Tere ishq ch pagal haan, out of my mind
Main sab kuj chhad ditta, leaving the world behind

[Verse 2]
Thandiyan raatan vich teri yaad maardi ae, freezing cold
Main darda ni maut ton, brave and bold
Zehriley is ishq nu main pi lenda haan, tasting the poison
Main sachhi yaari nibhda haan, the only reason
Mainu hor kise di lor nahi, you are enough
Main agg te chalda haan, journey is tough
Jadon tu rooth jaandi ae, losing my way
Main tere pichhe aunda haan, begging to stay

Main hasda haan sab agge, crying in rain
Par tu meri rooh vich enni dhoongi ae, deeply rooted
Main poojda haan tenu sachchi, completely undisputed
Sadi akh milan nu vekh loki sarde ne, making them stare
Main sirf tera ranjha haan, taking full care

[Bridge]
Main Saeed karda haan ikko fariyaad, pure romance
Tu meri ban jaave is janam vich, taking a chance
Tere saaha nal mere saah chalde ne, feeling complete
Main thehar jaanda haan tenu takk ke, losing my beat

[Final Chorus]
